welcome to typing speed Test

This application is intended to measure how quickly you type the English language on the keyboard. You will see a word that you must enter in the entry field that will appear to you, but this must be within the time period specified for you. The evaluation is done by the number of correct words you entered, and if you enter the word incorrectly, the evaluation will end.
